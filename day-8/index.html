Festival notice

Uttrayan Festival

The Uttrayan Festival will be celebrated from 03 March to 05 March 2018. During this period, various cultural events and activities will take place across the city. Residents are encouraged to participate and enjoy the festivities.

Public Holiday Announcement

In celebration of the Uttrayan Festival, 04 March 2018 has been declared a public holiday. All government offices and schools will remain closed on this day. We wish everyone a joyful and safe festival!

Maha Shivratri Celebration

Maha Shivratri will be observed on 13 February 2018. Devotees are invited to participate in special prayers and rituals at local temples. May Lord Shiva bless everyone with health and prosperity.
